About Us

At CM Baking, we aim to provide you with your baking needs for every occasion. From Wedding Cakes and Birthday Cakes and even Afternoon Tea, at CM Baking we're here to help with the best baking you'll find this side of Paris!!!

Founded in 2017 by retired Home Economics teaching duo Celaine and Maria, CM Baking was a way for both to continue on with their love of baking whilst providing a service for customers who were looking for that bespoke cake to celebrate whatever occasion. Since then, the business has grown to allow them to provide home baking, tray bakes and afternoon tea on request. Why not view our Cakes section to see some examples of our cakes which taste every bit as good as they look!
